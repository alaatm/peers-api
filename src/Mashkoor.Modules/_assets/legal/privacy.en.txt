<p> 
<strong>Scope and Application</strong> 
</p> 
<p>This privacy policy (this “privacy policy") applies to persons anywhere in the 
world who access or use our Services (“Users”) as well as to drivers, partner transportation

</li>

<p> 
<strong>Collection of Information</strong>

<em>Information You Provide to Us</em> 
</p> 
<p>We collect information you provide directly to us through your access or use of 
the Services, such as when you create or modify your account, request on-demand 
services, contact customer support, or otherwise communicate with us. This information

<p>Please note that we do not knowingly collect, keep or maintain personal information

</p> 
<p>When you use our Services, we collect personal information (i.e. information that

<p>We also use GPS technology to determine your current location. Some of our location-enabled

the particular feature, you will be asked to consent to your data being used for this purpose.</p>
